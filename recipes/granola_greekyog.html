Granola & Greek Yoghurt

Description

An easy, healthy breakfast or lunch.

Ingredients

  • Your favourite granola from the supermarket
  • Fage full fat Greek yoghurt
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • A handful of frozen fruit e.g. raspberries, strawberries, blueberries

Steps

  1. Fill a 1/4 of a bowl with your granola
  2. Fill another 1/4 with yoghurt
  3. Add your frozen fruit
  4. Drizzle the honey on top
