Rosie's Cheese Toastie

Description

A simple, satisfying cheese toastie.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of seeded farm bread
  • 10g butter
  • 20g mature cheddar cheese
  • 20g emmental cheese
  • 10g gouda cheese
  • 1 tsp parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp ketchup

Steps

  1. Spread half of the butter on one side of a slice of bread
  2. Face this butter-side down in a frying pan
  3. Add a layer of cheddar cheese on top
  4. Add the next layer of gouda
  5. Add the emmental
  6. Sprinkle the parmesan on top as a final layer
  7. Place the 2nd slice of bread on top
  8. Butter the top of the bread (the butter should be on the outside of the toastie)
  9. Fry at a low heat for 5-6 minutes, flipping occasionally
  10. When golden brown on both sides, cut into triangles using a spatula
  11. Let the cheese melt out and crisp a little at the edges
  12. Serve with ketchup and enjoy!
