Oat Iced Coffee

Description

A refreshing iced coffee with oat milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp of instant coffee
  • 1 tsp demerera sugar
  • Boiling water
  • Oat milk
  • 4-5 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add the coffee and sugar to a mug or heat-proof cup
  2. Add a small amount of boiling water
  3. Stir until dissolved
  4. Add the ice cubes to a glass
  5. Fill 3/4 glass with oat milk
  6. Pour the hot coffee on top
  7. Top with cold water if needed
  8. Stir to mix
  9. Bon appetit!
